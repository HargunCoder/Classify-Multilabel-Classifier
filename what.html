Classifie-Multi Label Text Classifier

Communication on the social forums is one of the most utilized methods of online conversation and expressing oneself today. Discussing things and giving opinions over particular thing or post can be difficult. On these platforms’ abuses, online harassment, jeering and cyber-bullying over comments can have negative impact on a person's psychological health and life. It leads to many people stop expressing themselves and give up on seeking different opinions. the organizations who provides such online discussion platforms have worked on such comment classification and found out classifying the comments based on their toxicity into multiple categories to call for appropriate actions to curb the negative influences on social platforms. Such a multi-label classification model will make the purpose of social media more constructive and positive also helping the companies save time and efforts in moderating their platforms. For this task, Convolution Neural Network (CNN) has shown more efficient results in sentiment analysis, for our model, we have used Kaggle’s Toxic Comment Classification Dataset regarding Wikipedia's talk page edits to develop a multi-headed model which classifies the comments based on the toxicity of comments as toxic, severe toxic, obscene, threat, insult, identity hate. We aim to apply the text-based Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) With word embedding, using Fast Text Method to analyse the comments. Our model will aim to improve on detecting different types of toxicity to improvise the social media experience.
